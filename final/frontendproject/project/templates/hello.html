What To Expect

When you enter a stock symbol on our home page, multiple tabs will open. Data displayed will inform a user on how the stock is performing and which stocks to potentially invest in based on its historical pricing trend, its correlation to the stock index, and select risk metrics (e.g. bollinger band).
